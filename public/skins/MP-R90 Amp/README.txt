- MP-R90 Amp -

  based on: SONY Portable Minidisc Recorder MZ-R90 - a picture of this
  is included, called mz-r90.jpg.
- 58% Original skin. 3% ideas from other skins, 18% ideas because of
  limitations from placement of buttons in Winamp, 21% ideas from my
  Sony MZ-R90 MD player

a skin for WinAMP 2.6 or higher. Includes Playlist, EQ, MiniBrowser,
and AVS skins.

by Victor Li - victorli23@hotmail.com

version 1.0 - 04.05.00 - this is the first and probably last version,
because I didn't release it until it was as perfect as I could be
bothered to make it. If you would like to improve on it, go ahead,
but make sure you send me a copy because I'd love to see it.

I bought a Sony mz-r90 for christmas 99 - I got my friend to buy it for
me from Hong Kong. It's a great little machine, although a bit pricy...
The skin took ages to make, especially since it was all done from
scratch, after scanning in my MD player. Of course, I cannibalised a
few other skins to get the MZ-R90 skin just right, and for those bits
and pieces that were just too tedious to do myself. My greatest
achievement in making this skin was figuring out that the colours
specified in the PLedit.txt file were in hexadecimal. This project
took probably 20 hours to make, in total. This was the result of being
bored in the school holidays. blah blah blah blah...
Enjoy the skin!
